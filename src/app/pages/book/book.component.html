Drum's House

Come meet Drum, a little Tom-tom who invites you into his home to meet his whole family. His mom is a Xylophone and his dad is a Snare Drum and that is just the beginning.

Drum's House is an imaginative children's book, written in rhymes, by Noya Schleien, that teaches children about the world of Percussion Instruments. The cool rhythmic instruments come to life as characters in the family and as objects in the house.

Throughout the book, you can find QR codes. Scanning these codes leads to a series of videos introducing the Percussion Instruments with all of their charm!

The book is in now available in Hebrew.

Recommended for children aged 4-8.
